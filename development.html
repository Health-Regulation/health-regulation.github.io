Quick guide

  • Checklist feature: Check off items on the checklist as you complete them to ensure safety of your AI model for healthcare use.
  • References to AIGHIe guidelines: The reference to the original AIGHIe guidelines' section and subsection can be found in square brackets (e.g. [4.2.1]).
  • Expand for more details: Tap on the icon to find summative details extracted from the appropriate parts within the original AIGHIe guidelines.
  • Generate report feature: Generate brief summary of uncompleted tasks.
